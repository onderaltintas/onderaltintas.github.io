You Are Awesome Android App (30.03.2025)

The Story of the App: Google sends a series of emails to developers, informing them that inactive accounts in the Google Play Developer program will be deleted. To prevent deletion, you either need to upload a new app or update an existing one. The deadline is April 4th. With time running out, I rushed to create a rather unpolished app and uploaded it just 4 days before the deadline. After that, thanks to ChatGPT, I had a song lyric written, used Suno AI to make a piece of music, and added it to the app. ChatGPT also provided the code for adding videos. At this point, I think AI should just take over our jobs. 😊
